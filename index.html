Don't Trust, Validate

What are
our Services?

Security
Engagements

From penetration tests of company networks to fine-tuned code audits, we cover a wide area of topics. By combining our own methodology with industry standards and precise scoping we ensure that every engagement delivers actionable results for exploitable vulnerabilities not just compliance issues. Drawing from our own research we constantly try to reassess how and what vulnerabilities need to be considered while providing applicable counter measures to ensure that customers are prepared for securing their systems.

Forensic &
Incident Response

When faced with security breaches we assist customers in identifying and prioritizing measures for ensuring operational continuity and minimizing damage. Working closely with our customers at every step and helping them gain insight to make effective decisions is our top priority. After incidents, analysis for indicators of compromise help address previous and future attacks. Since prevention is better than mitigation, we can also aid customers in assessing their incident strategies and playbooks before an attack happens.

Threat
Analysis

Using our experience in thread modeling and security workshops can help customers close knowledge gaps and understand their specific risk exposure. If systems were grown over time vulnerabilities and blind spots can pervade throughout an organization which makes working with all the different stake holders key. Verifying if security know-how and internal guidelines provide adequate protection against threats and comparing them with all available best practices is key for our holistic approach to security.

Challenging assumptions
lies at the heart of security

nullFaktor is an independent security and research company with an affinity for complex SAP environments. Our mission is to help customers identify and mitigate critical vulnerabilities in their systems starting all the way from the drawing board down to the core infrastructure. Having worked in the industry with companies of all sizes we learned that security flaws don't just stop at the authorization level but permeate throughout all parts of IT including processes and people. As a company we believe that real security implies constant learning and that our work should deliver clear facts, absent of whitewashing to enable our customers to reassess their security posture.
